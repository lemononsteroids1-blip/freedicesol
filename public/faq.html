How it works
How do I play? ▼
Connect your wallet, pick a game, enter a bet and hit the button. Win and SOL goes straight back to your wallet.
What wallets are supported? ▼
Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, Glow, Coinbase, Trust, Brave and Exodus.
What is the minimum bet? ▼
0.001 SOL. You can also bet 0 to try for free.
What is the house edge? ▼
98% RTP on all games.
Which blockchain does FreeDice use? ▼
Solana.
Games
How does Dice work? ▼
Pick a number, roll over or under it. Higher risk = higher multiplier.
How does Blackjack work? ▼
Get closer to 21 than the dealer without busting. Hit, Stand, Double or Split.
How does Coin Flip work? ▼
Heads or tails. Win = 1.98x your bet.
How does Plinko work? ▼
Ball drops through pegs and lands in a multiplier slot. Pick your risk level and row count.
Referral Program
How does the referral program work? ▼
Share your link. Anyone who plays through it earns you 10% of the house edge on every bet they place.
When do I get paid? ▼
Earnings build up and you claim whenever you want.
Is there a limit on referral earnings? ▼
No cap, no expiry.
